Outlier detection (Tukey's fences): For each row or column, the interquartile range (IQR = Q3 − Q1) is calculated. Values below Q1 − 1.5 × IQR or above Q3 + 1.5 × IQR are flagged as outliers and marked with a black dot. This is a standard non-parametric method that does not assume normality.
